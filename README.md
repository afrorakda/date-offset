# Date Offset | Fridge Combo #77

A hyper-minimalist tool for professional accountants, legal staff, and project managers to calculate target dates with zero friction. No calendar scrolling, no heavy UI—just numbers and results.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Enter a base date and an offset (days/months/years) to get your target date instantly.
- **Brain-Dead Entry**: Smart logic that understands 4, 6, or 8-digit date inputs.
- **Speed First**: Quick-tap presets for Today, Tomorrow, and common business offsets (7/30/90 days).

## How to Use
1. **Base Date**: Enter the starting point.
   - `0405` → April 5th (Current Year)
   - `260405` → April 5th, 2026 (YYMMDD)
   - `040526` → April 5th, 2026 (MMDDYY)
   - `20260405` → April 5th, 2026 (YYYYMMDD)
2. **Offset**: Enter the number of days, months, or years to add or subtract.
   - e.g., `45` to find a deadline, `-15` to find a past date.
3. **Copy**: Tap the result card to copy the formatted date to your clipboard.
